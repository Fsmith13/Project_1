Take a look at the NBA All Star voting returns!

The first returns of the 2019 NBA All Star Game votes have been released. The voting for the NBA All Star Game consists of votes from fans, current NBA players, and a media panel. The fan votes account for 50% of the vote to determine the 10 starters in the 2019 NBA All Star Game. Current NBA players and a media panel both account for 25% of the vote each. Each voter votes on three frontcourt players, and two guards from each conference. All Star voting ends on Monday, January the 21st at 11:59 p.m EST. The All Star starters and captains will be announced on Thursday, January 24th during TNT NBA Tip Off.
